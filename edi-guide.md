# Points of interest
### Cramond Island
https://maps.app.goo.gl/3a9VjfkGZT2gPtvV8
You can get in and out of there during a low tide, when the path is above water. Just a chill place, with some old fortifications on the causeway, and some old buildings which possibly are used as drug dens xD it is preferable to visit during the day
